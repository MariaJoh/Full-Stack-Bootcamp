Latte

Ingredients: Espresso + steamed milk + light milk foam
How it’s made:
1. Pull a shot (or two) of espresso.
2. Steam milk until it’s hot and has a silky texture.
3. Pour the steamed milk into the espresso, holding back most of the foam.
4. Top with a thin layer of milk foam.
Ratio: ~1 part espresso : 4–6 parts steamed milk.
Creamy, smooth, and milk-forward.


Cappuccino

Ingredients: Espresso + steamed milk + thick milk foam
How it’s made:
1. Pull a shot of espresso.
2. Steam milk so it has more foam (lighter, airier texture than a latte).
3. Pour the steamed milk over the espresso, aiming for equal parts espresso, milk, and foam.
Ratio: ~1 part espresso : 1 part steamed milk : 1 part foam.
Stronger coffee flavor than a latte, with a fluffy texture.


Americano

Ingredients: Espresso + hot water
How it’s made:
1. Pull a shot (or two) of espresso.
2. Add hot water on top until it reaches the desired strength.
Ratio: ~1 part espresso : 2–3 parts hot water.
Tastes similar to drip coffee but with espresso’s richer flavor.
